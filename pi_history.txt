[Create Gunicorn systemd Service File]
sudo nano /etc/systemd/system/gunicorn.service
`paste the following:`
"""
[Unit]
Description=gunicorn daemon
After=network.target


[Service]
User=pi
Group=www-data
WorkingDirectory=/home/pi/Developer/cos_project
ExecStart=/home/pi/.virtualenvs/inv_prod/bin/gunicorn --access-logfile - --workers 3 --bind unix:/home/pi/Developer/cos_project/inventory.sock inventory.wsgi:application

[Install]
WantedBy=multi-user.target
"""
